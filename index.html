Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters: SplitXpense is designed with privacy at its core. All your expense data stays on your device, and we never store your personal financial information on our servers.

Introduction

SplitXpense ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application SplitXpense (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • User Profile Information: Name, email address (optional)
  • Expense Data: Receipt images, expense amounts, descriptions, categories
  • Group Information: Group names, member information
  • Payment Information: Payment status and settlement data

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • App Usage Data: Feature usage, crash reports, performance metrics
  • Camera and Photo Library Access: Only when you choose to scan receipts or select images
  • AI Processing: Receipt images are sent to Google Gemini AI for text extraction using your personal API key

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Financial Information: We do not collect or store bank account details, credit card numbers, or other sensitive financial data
  • Location Data: We do not collect your precise location
  • Third-Party Data: We do not collect information from third-party sources
  • Cloud Storage: We do not sync or store your data in the cloud
  • Push Notifications: We do not send push notifications

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • App Functionality: To provide expense tracking and bill splitting features
  • Receipt Processing: To scan and extract information from receipt images using Google Gemini AI with your API key
  • Group Management: To manage expense groups and member relationships
  • Payment Tracking: To track payment status and settlements

Google Gemini AI Integration

Important: When you scan receipts, the images are processed by Google Gemini AI using your personal API key. This means:

  • You control your own Google Gemini API key
  • Receipt processing happens through your direct relationship with Google
  • We do not have access to your API key or the processed data
  • You can revoke or change your API key at any time

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Priority

  • Primary Storage: All your data is stored locally on your device
  • Core Data: We use Apple's Core Data framework for secure local storage
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted using iOS system encryption
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not sync your data to iCloud or any cloud service

Security Measures

  • Device Security: We rely on your device's built-in security features
  • No Server Storage: We do not store your data on our servers
  • Secure Transmission: Any data transmission uses industry-standard encryption

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT Share Your Data

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
  • No Advertising: We do not use your data for advertising purposes
  • No Analytics: We do not share usage data with analytics companies

Limited Exceptions

  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law
  • App Store: Basic usage statistics may be shared with Apple for App Store analytics
  • Your Consent: We will only share data with your explicit consent

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Modification: You can edit or delete your expense data at any time
  • Export: You can export your data from the app
  • Deletion: You can delete the app to remove all local data

App Permissions

  • Camera: Required for receipt scanning (can be disabled in device settings)
  • Photo Library: Required for selecting receipt images (can be disabled in device settings)

Children's Privacy

  • Age Requirement: Our app is not intended for children under 13
  • No Collection: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Parental Control: If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us

International Users

  • Local Storage: Your data remains on your local device
  • No International Transfer: We do not transfer data internationally
  • Local Laws: We comply with applicable local privacy laws

Your GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Restriction: Request restriction of processing
